Meet your best friend
who actually gets you.

It Connects, Remembers, Feels, and Acts, All in One Conversation.

A new paradigm of AI interaction. Purpose-built for emotional depth, persistent memory, and agentic capabilities at its core, Iyomi fundamentally redefines what it means to have a conversation with a machine and why it should feel like more than just a tool.

FIG 0.1

Built for connection

Shaped by the psychology of human bonding persistent memory, tonal awareness, and a personality that never breaks character.

FIG 0.2

Powered by MCP agents

Designed for workflows where conversation becomes action. From checking your calendar to managing your day through natural dialogue.

FIG 0.3

Engineered for presence

Sub-3-second voice latency, emotion-adaptive TTS, and silence-aware turn-taking that eliminates the uncanny valley of AI conversation.

Iyomi chat lets you think out
loud without losing context.

Understands how your thoughts evolve over time,
keeping track of context, recalling past conversations,
and responding in a way that feels continuous rather
than reset with every message.

Iyomi voice lets you talk naturally
and be understood instantly.

Enables real-time voice interactions that feel
fluid and responsive, adjusting tone, pacing,
and emotional nuance so conversations feel
natural and comfortable instead of mechanical.

Iyomi actions lets you get
things done just by talking.

Transforms conversation into meaningful outcomes
by managing tasks, coordinating multiple steps,
and executing workflows in the background while
you stay focused on the conversation itself.
